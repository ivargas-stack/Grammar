Master the Past Continuous Tense
Active vs. Passive Voice

How to Play

PLAYER 1

ACTIVE VOICE

The "Doer" does the action.

Formula:

was/were + verb-ing

Example:

"The tiger was chasing the guide."

PLAYER 2

PASSIVE VOICE

The "Receiver" comes first!

Formula:

was/were + being + V3

Example:

"The guide was being chased!"
